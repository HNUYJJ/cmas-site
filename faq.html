大会什么时候、在哪里举办？When and where will the conference be held?

第四届中国多智能体系统大会将于 2026 年 6 月 27-28 日在南京大学苏州校区举办。The 4th China Multi-Agent Systems Conference will be held on June 27-28, 2026 at the Suzhou Campus of Nanjing University.

本届会议由谁主办？Who organizes CMAS 2026?

大会由 CCF 主办，CCFAI 和南京大学协办，CCFAI 多智能体学组执行。The conference is hosted by CCF, co-organized by CCFAI and Nanjing University, and executed by the CCFAI Multi-Agent Systems Group.

本届有哪些分论坛？What forums are included this year?

六个分论坛分别为：大模型智能体的记忆系统、多智能体强化学习、博弈论与机制设计、具身智能、从模型训练到科学发现：智能体技术的前沿探索、多智能体与社会科学交叉研究。The six forums are memory systems for LLM agents, multi-agent reinforcement learning, game theory and mechanism design, embodied intelligence, from model training to scientific discovery, and multi-agent systems with social science.

企业如何参与合作？How can companies join as partners?

大会欢迎企业合作伙伴参与赞助、展示和产学研交流，相关说明已经整理在“联系我们”页面。Companies are welcome to join through sponsorship, exhibition, and industry-academia exchange, and the related guidance is organized on the Contact page.

历届会议新闻在哪里查看？Where can I find news of past editions?

可在“历届会议”页面查看 CMAS 2023、2024、2025 及 DAI 2025 的公众号链接。You can find the WeChat links for CMAS 2023, 2024, 2025, and DAI 2025 on the History page.
