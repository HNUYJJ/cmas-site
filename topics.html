本届大会将设置六个分论坛，分别由来自天津大学、上海交通大学、南方科技大学、南京大学、浦江实验室、华东师范大学与同济大学的专家组织。This edition will feature six forums organized by experts from Tianjin University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, SUSTech, Nanjing University, Pujiang Lab, East China Normal University, and Tongji University.

ForumsForums

六个分论坛Six Forums

论坛名称已经按会务说明落位，后续如有微调可以直接在此页面替换。The forum titles are now arranged according to the conference brief and can be adjusted here later if needed.

论坛 1：大模型智能体的记忆系统Forum 1: Memory Systems for LLM Agents

组织者：天津大学郑岩。聚焦大模型智能体的记忆建模、长期状态维护与任务协作。Organizer: Yan Zheng, Tianjin University. Focuses on memory modeling, long-term state maintenance, and task collaboration for LLM agents.

论坛 2：多智能体强化学习Forum 2: Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning

组织者：上海交通大学温睦宁。围绕多智能体协同决策、训练范式与复杂环境中的 MARL 展开。Organizer: Muning Wen, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Covers cooperative decision-making, training paradigms, and MARL in complex environments.

论坛 3：博弈论与机制设计Forum 3: Game Theory and Mechanism Design

组织者：南方科技大学孔芳、南京理工大学李斌。关注策略交互、机制设计与理性决策。Organizers: Fang Kong, SUSTech; Bin Li, Nanjing University of Science and Technology. Focuses on strategic interaction, mechanism design, and rational decision-making.

论坛 4：具身智能Forum 4: Embodied Intelligence

组织者：南京大学霍静。关注具身智能体在感知、行动和协作任务中的最新进展。Organizer: Jing Huo, Nanjing University. Highlights the latest advances in perception, action, and collaboration for embodied agents.

论坛 5：从模型训练到科学发现：智能体技术的前沿探索Forum 5: From Model Training to Scientific Discovery: Frontiers of Agent Technologies

组织者：浦江实验室胡舒悦。对应 AI4S 方向，关注智能体技术对科学发现的支撑。Organizer: Shuyue Hu, Pujiang Lab. Corresponds to AI4S and explores how agent technologies support scientific discovery.

论坛 6：多智能体与社会科学交叉研究Forum 6: Multi-Agent Systems and Social Science

组织者：华东师范大学钱鸿、王祥丰，同济大学李文浩。对应 AI4Social Science 方向。Organizers: Hong Qian and Xiangfeng Wang, East China Normal University; Wenhao Li, Tongji University. Corresponds to AI4Social Science.
