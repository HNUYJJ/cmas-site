会议介绍About CCMAS 2026

CCF多智能体系统会议（CCMAS）是国内多智能体领域的重要年度学术会议，面向高校、科研机构与产业界，聚焦多智能体学习、具身智能、博弈与机制设计、科学发现智能体等前沿方向。CCMAS 2026 将围绕“基础理论-关键技术-产业落地”组织主旨报告、专题论坛与交流活动，推动学术创新与产业协同。The CCF Multi-Agent Systems Conference (CCMAS) is a key annual event for the domestic MAS community, connecting academia, research institutes, and industry. CCMAS 2026 focuses on frontier areas including multi-agent learning, embodied intelligence, game theory and mechanism design, and agent-driven scientific discovery, with a program spanning keynotes, thematic forums, and networking sessions.
