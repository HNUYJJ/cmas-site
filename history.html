历届会议Conference History

中国多智能体系统大会自 2023 年起已连续举办三届，先后落地长沙、太原、西安，2026 年将来到南京大学苏州校区。Since 2023, CMAS has been held for three consecutive editions in Changsha, Taiyuan, and Xi'an, and will arrive at the Suzhou Campus of Nanjing University in 2026

会议时间线Conference Timeline

多智能体学组已发展为 CCFAI 专委下规模最大、最活跃的学术组织，CMAS 也逐步成长为全国影响力最大的多智能体学术会议之一。The Multi-Agent Systems Group has become the largest and most active academic organization under CCFAI, and CMAS has grown into one of the most influential multi-agent conferences in China

2026

第四届中国多智能体系统会议。The 4th China Multi-Agent Systems Conference

2026 年 6 月 27-28 日 - 南京大学苏州校区。June 27-28, 2026 - Suzhou Campus of Nanjing University

  • 由 CCF 主办，CCFAI 和南京大学协办，多智能体学组执行Hosted by CCF, co-organized by CCFAI and Nanjing University, and executed by the Multi-Agent Systems Group
  • 设主旨报告及六个分论坛，并安排赞助商讲话、晚宴Features keynote talks, six forums, sponsor remarks, a banquet, and awards
2025

第三届中国多智能体系统会议。The 3rd China Multi-Agent Systems Conference

2025 年 - 西安，西安电子科技大学联合承办。2025 - Xi'an, co-hosted by Xidian University

  • 第三届会议延续了 CMAS 的全国影响力，进一步扩大了多智能体系统学术共同体The third edition continued the nationwide influence of CMAS and further expanded the multi-agent systems community
  • 历届大会参会规模已达到 350 人，CMAS 逐步成长为国内最具代表性的多智能体会议The conference scale has reached 350 attendees across recent editions, and CMAS has become one of the most representative meetings in the field in China
2024

第二届中国多智能体系统会议。The 2nd China Multi-Agent Systems Conference

2024 年 - 太原，山西大学联合承办。2024 - Taiyuan, co-hosted by Shanxi University

  • 第二届会议进一步扩大了大会规模，成为多智能体系统领域的重要年度交流平台The second edition further expanded the conference scale and strengthened its role as a major annual exchange platform
  • 会议由山西大学联合承办，延续了 CMAS 跨区域协同举办的组织模式Co-hosted by Shanxi University, the edition continued the cross-regional organizational model of CMAS
2023

第一届中国多智能体系统会议。The 1st China Multi-Agent Systems Conference

2023 年 - 长沙，国防科技大学联合承办。2023 - Changsha, co-hosted by the National University of Defense Technology

  • CMAS 自 2023 年起正式形成年度大会机制，开启国内多智能体系统会议品牌建设CMAS established its annual conference mechanism in 2023 and started building a domestic conference brand for multi-agent systems
  • 长沙首届会议奠定了此后太原、西安与苏州连续举办的基础The inaugural conference in Changsha laid the foundation for the subsequent editions in Taiyuan, Xi'an, and Suzhou
