往届回顾Past Editions

中国多智能体系统大会自 2023 年起已连续举办三届，先后落地长沙、太原、西安，2026 年将来到南京大学苏州校区。Since 2023, CCMAS has been held for three consecutive editions in Changsha, Taiyuan, and Xi'an, and will arrive at the Suzhou Campus of Nanjing University in 2026.

会议时间线Conference Timeline

多智能体学组已发展为 CCFAI 专委下规模最大、最活跃的学术组织，CCMAS 也逐步成长为全国影响力最大的多智能体学术会议之一。The Multi-Agent Systems Group has become one of the largest and most active academic communities under CCFAI, and CCMAS has grown into one of the most influential multi-agent conferences in China.

苏州，南京大学苏州校区承办Suzhou, hosted at NJU Suzhou Campus

  • 设置主旨报告、分论坛、赞助商讲话与晚宴等核心环节Featuring keynotes, forum sessions, sponsor talks, and banquet activities

西安，西安电子科技大学联合承办Xi'an, co-hosted by Xidian University

2024

第二届中国多智能体系统大会The 2nd CCMAS

太原，山西大学联合承办Taiyuan, co-hosted by Shanxi University

2023

第一届中国多智能体系统大会The 1st CCMAS

长沙，国防科技大学联合承办Changsha, co-hosted by NUDT

  • 自 2023 年起正式形成年度大会机制，开启国内多智能体会议品牌建设CCMAS established its annual conference mechanism in 2023 and started building a sustained conference brand in China
  • 长沙首届会议奠定后续太原、西安与苏州连续举办基础The inaugural Changsha edition laid the foundation for subsequent editions in Taiyuan, Xi'an, and Suzhou
