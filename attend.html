交通方式Transportation

  • 高铁：建议到达苏州站或苏州北站，再换乘地铁/出租车至会场。High-speed rail: Arrive at Suzhou Railway Station or Suzhou North, then transfer by metro or taxi.
  • 机场：可从苏南硕放机场或上海虹桥机场抵达后转高铁。Airports: Fly to Sunan Shuofang or Shanghai Hongqiao, then transfer by high-speed rail.
  • 市内交通：会议期间建议优先网约车，早晚高峰预留 20-30 分钟机动时间。City transit: Ride-hailing is recommended during the conference; reserve 20-30 minutes buffer at peak hours.
  • 报到提示：6 月 26 日报到，建议提前完成酒店入住后到会场领取资料。Check-in tip: Registration opens on June 26; complete hotel check-in first and then collect conference materials.
